Our Services

What We Offer

Comprehensive ultrasound imaging services delivered with precision and care.

🦴

Musculoskeletal Ultrasound

Ultrasound assessment of joints and soft tissues, including shoulders, knees, ankles, tendons, ligaments, and muscles. Commonly used for sports injuries, pain, inflammation, or limited movement.

🫁

Abdominal Ultrasound

Comprehensive ultrasound imaging of abdominal organs, performed with accuracy, care, and attention to patient comfort. Detailed evaluation for accurate clinical diagnosis.

🫀

Vascular Ultrasound

Specialised ultrasound studies to assess blood flow and circulation, including carotid, venous, and arterial scans. Essential for diagnosing vascular conditions.
