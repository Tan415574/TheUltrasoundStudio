The Human Lens

Expertise Guided by Empathy.

I obtained my National Diploma in Radiography from the Central University of Technology (CUT) in 2001 and my BTech in Ultrasound from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT) in 2003. Over the past 20 years, I've worked in private radiology practices in both Cape Town and Bloemfontein.

At The Ultrasound Studio, my focus is on delivering high-quality diagnostic ultrasound examinations in a calm, professional, and caring environment. I believe excellent imaging goes hand-in-hand with patient comfort, clear communication, and a personal approach.
