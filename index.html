My Background

Dedicated to Quality Diagnostic Imaging

I obtained my National Diploma in Radiography from the Central University of Technology (CUT) in 2001 and my BTech in Ultrasound from the Cape Peninsula University of Technology (CPUT) in 2003. Over the past 20 years, I've gained extensive experience working in private radiology practices in both Cape Town and Bloemfontein.

At The Ultrasound Studio, my focus is on delivering high-quality diagnostic ultrasound examinations in a calm, professional, and caring environment. I believe that excellent imaging goes hand-in-hand with patient comfort, clear communication, and a personal approach — ensuring every patient feels supported and well cared for.

What We Offer

Our Diagnostic
Ultrasound Services

At The Ultrasound Studio, we offer a range of general diagnostic ultrasound examinations performed with precision, care, and a personal approach. All scans are conducted in a calm and professional environment, with patient comfort as a priority.

01

Musculoskeletal Ultrasound

Ultrasound assessment of joints and soft tissues. Commonly used for sports injuries, pain, inflammation, or limited movement.

  • Shoulders & rotator cuff
  • Knees & ankles
  • Tendons & ligaments
  • Muscles & soft tissue
Musculoskeletal
02

Abdominal Ultrasound

An ultrasound examination of the abdominal organs. Commonly used to investigate abdominal pain, swelling, or abnormal blood results.

  • Liver & gallbladder
  • Pancreas & spleen
  • Kidneys & bladder
  • Abdominal aorta
Abdominal
03

Small Parts Ultrasound

High-resolution imaging of smaller anatomical areas. These examinations assist in evaluating lumps, swelling, or areas of concern.

  • Breast
  • Thyroid
  • Scrotum
Small Parts
04

Vascular Ultrasound

Non-invasive ultrasound studies to assess blood flow and circulation. Used to investigate circulation problems, blockages, or vascular disease.

  • Carotid artery studies
  • Venous studies (e.g. DVT assessment)
  • Arterial studies
Vascular
05

Pelvic & Gynaecological Ultrasound

Assessment of the pelvic organs, including the uterus, ovaries, and surrounding structures. Used to evaluate pelvic pain, menstrual irregularities, or other gynaecological concerns.

  • Uterus & endometrium
  • Ovaries & adnexa
  • Pelvic pain investigation
  • Menstrual irregularities
Pelvic
06

Soft Tissue Ultrasound

Evaluation of superficial soft tissue areas, including lumps, bumps, cysts, and swellings. This scan helps determine the nature of soft tissue abnormalities.

  • Lumps & bumps
  • Cysts & lipomas
  • Superficial swellings
  • Soft tissue masses
Soft Tissue
